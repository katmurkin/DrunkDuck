Welcome to the Drunk Duck webmap. This page is designed to allow you to find points of interest along the canal network. Currently it includes information about the canal network around Birmingham, with points of interest being limited to restaurants and pubs along the canals.

You can search for a canal using the dropdown menu to the left, or by clicking on or near one of the canals on the map.
